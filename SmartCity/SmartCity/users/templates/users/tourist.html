Brisbane Hotel Information

Stamford Plaza

Corner of Margaret Street, Brisbane
Location: Located in Brisbane Central Business District, this luxury hotel is within a 10-minute walk of Brisbane Botanic Gardens, Eagle Street Pier, and Queen Street Mall. Museum of Brisbane is 0.5 mi (0.8 km) away.

Hotel Features: This hotel features 3 restaurants, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center. Additionally, a bar/lounge, a spa tub, and a sauna are onsite.
​
Room Amenities: All 252 rooms feature thoughtful touches like bathrobes and slippers, plus 24-hour room service and plasma TVs with digital channels. Other amenities available to guests include WiFi, wired Internet, and minibars.

Marriott Hotel

515 Queen Street, Brisbane
Centrally located: Conveniently located in the downtown Brisbane, just 1 Km from Queen Street Mall and 1 block from riverside bars & dining, markets, art galleries, nightlife and the financial heart of Brisbane.

Comprehensive business facilities: Specialising in business, corporate, social and wedding events with conference facilities and a full business center. Relaxation can be found by the pool or in the spa and sauna.
​
Premium bedding: Beds in the 263 luxurious guestrooms and 4 suites are topped with premium Marriott signature bedding; all guestrooms have city views. Innovative 'plug in and play’ technology, 32-inch LCD TV, and Marriott’s own aromatherapy amenities.

​

Treasury Brisbane

130 William Street, Brisbane
Location: Located in Brisbane Central Business District, this luxury hotel is steps from Treasury Casino and Commissariat Store Museum. Queen Street Mall and Museum of Brisbane are also within 10 minutes.

Hotel Features: 4 restaurants, a casino, and a nightclub are available at this hotel. WiFi in public areas is free. Other amenities include 4 bars/lounges, a fitness center, and a coffee shop/café.

​Room Amenities: All 127 rooms offer free WiFi and free wired Internet, LCD TVs with cable channels, and comforts like pillowtop beds and premium bedding. Other amenities available to guests include iPod docks, 24-hour room service, and refrigerators.
